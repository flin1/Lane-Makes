FAQs

What is a makeathon?

A makeathon is an 24 hour event where anyone can create anything. A makeathon is meant to inspire and also help teach you new skills. Makeathons are also a good way to build a portfolio and practice creating.

Who can attend?

Make@Lane is for high school students located around the Chicagoland area.
