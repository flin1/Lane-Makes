About Make @ Lane

It is a 24 hour makeathon where you can create anything. Anything you want. It can be a website, a craft, or a wacky machine. Anything that fits the theme of the event. This year the theme is Social Good. Any project that you believe will help the world in some way or solve an ongoing problem in the world.

What is a makeathon?

A makeathon is an 24 hour event where anyone can create anything. A makeathon is meant to inspire and also help teach you new skills. Makeathons are also a good way to build a portfolio and practice creating.
